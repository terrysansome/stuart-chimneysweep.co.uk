About Stuart Adams Chimney Sweep

Do you require the services of a chimney sweep?

Then look no further than Stuart Adams, Chimney Sweep. I’m a fully qualified member of The Institute of Chimney Sweeps (ICS) and being centrally located near Bicester, I work throughout Oxfordshire, Buckinghamshire, Northamptonshire and Berkshire.

Formerly of the Fireplace, Bicester, with 20 years’ experience I provide a professional, courteous and conscientious service, ensuring your stove or fireplace is a safe and a valuable addition to your home for years to come. Call 07595 660 867 or 01869 323481 to discuss your requirements.

Regular sweeping is essential to remove blockages and soot, preventing fires and allowing deadly combustion gases to be safely vented through the chimney. Clean chimneys burn more efficiently, which will save you money, as well as helping to preserve the environment. When you use an ICS registered chimney sweep you will receive a sweeping certificate, essential for insurance purposes, and your peace of mind.

How often should sweeping be carried out?

Smokeless Coals: Once a year. Wood: Twice a year when in use. Bitumous Coal: Twice a year. Oil: Once a year. Gas: Once a year.
